About AZËE

Montreal based Canadian DJ, producer and songwriter AZËE started to write and produce in 2016 after years of self-learning. He then bought some equipment and opened his first recording studio. Working with diverse genres — Hip Hop, RnB, Electronic Music, and Pop — he was able to build a large clientele and started to get some attention and recognition from Canadian artists.

After producing and engineering for quite a few talents during his first few years, AZËE then started to work on some personal projects. In 2020 he did a collaboration with one of the top Montreal producers' Ruffsound, on a song named "Baila" — a reggae electro-pop influenced track. The song reached the Top 100 BDS radio charts in Quebec.

In April 2022, AZËE decided to drop his first single as a Producer. After only his 2nd release, he got an amazing feature on the number one electronic music blog, EDM.com. Now in 2026 with more than 3 million streams total, AZËE is releasing new music every month — blending melodic dubstep with raw emotion in the spirit of ILLENIUM, building a sound that is uniquely his own.

With collaborations featuring singers such as Jazara, RUNN, Luma, and Chandler Leighton, as well as fellow Montreal-based producers JOSS, Gryxx, and Oddentrix, AZËE is an emerging producer who is consistently releasing music while continuously refining and perfecting his craft.
